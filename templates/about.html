About Fake Review Detector

The Fake Review Detector is an intelligent system designed to identify fake and misleading reviews on e-commerce platforms. It helps users and businesses make informed decisions by highlighting suspicious reviews automatically.

Purpose

  • Protect users from being misled by fake reviews.
  • Help businesses maintain credibility and trust.
  • Analyze review content to detect anomalies and patterns.

How It Works

The system uses advanced machine learning techniques to classify reviews as real or fake. It considers:

  • Text-based Features: review length, sentiment, punctuation, repeated words, spelling mistakes.
  • Reviewer-based Features: number of reviews by the user, account age, average rating.
  • Pattern Analysis: similarity with other reviews and unusual writing patterns.

Features

  • Detect fake reviews with high accuracy.
  • Display confusion matrix and classification metrics for transparency.
  • Provide explanations for why a review is flagged as fake.
  • User-friendly dashboard with easy navigation.

Future Enhancements

  • Incorporate more reviewer metadata for better detection.
  • Use advanced NLP models like BERT for higher accuracy.
  • Add real-time detection for live reviews.
